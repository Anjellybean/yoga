about

I am Anjali, a Punjabi-Acadian yoga teacher based in London. I was introduced to yoga through family but developed a deeper practice through a 200H yoga teacher training in Ashtanga Vinyasa in Goa, India. With yoga I learned practical tools that gave me the ability to turn inwards, move with awarness and cultivate a sense of calm that extends far beyond the mat and into day-to-day life. Sharing these ancient tools is a source of joy.

I am also a cultural producer, writer, researcher and DJ facilitating space for artists, happenings and collaborations that span nightlife, DIY publishing & community organising.
