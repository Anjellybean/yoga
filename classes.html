classes

My classes are warm, inclusive, and connect you with the core philosophies of yoga. Classes see you lead through a yoga flow of physical postures, breathwork and guided meditation. I focus on connecting movement with breath & alignment, giving you and understanding of the benefit of the postures, and aim to cultivate a deeper connection with the body, and through this, a deeper connection with the world around you.

what you can expect from a 1 hour class:

  • grounding/ intention setting
  • asana (physical postures)
  • pranayama (breathwork)
  • short guided meditation
